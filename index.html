Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 Cups Unsalted Butter (put in microwave for 10 seconds)
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 1 cup Dark Brown Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs (cold)
  • 1/3 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons Cake Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 3.5 cups Dark or Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Place one rack in middle of oven. Preheat the oven to 400 convection bake or 425 regular bake. Let the oven preheat for 20 minutes before baking.
  2. Cut butter in tablespoon size pieces and place into a stand mixer. Turn the mixer onto medium speed and beat the butter and sugars for a minute until the butter starts to mix with the sugar.
  3. Add eggs one at a time until mixed well. (Keep speeed on medium) Turn mixer off and add the cocoa powder mix until its all blended well. Scrap sides of bowl a few times while mixing.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips. Turn mixer down to low or use a spoon until the chocolate is mixed equally.
  5. Scoop the cookies onto a kitchen scale, they should weigh about 6oz each. There will be roughly 8-9 cookies after weighing them. If you want smaller cookies weigh until about 3 oz. Refrigerate before baking to keep the dough from spreading too much.
  6. The big cookies do not need to be cooled only the smaller ones. Put 4-5 big cookies on a baking sheet at a time.
  7. Bake for 5-7 minutes, you will know they're done when a crust has formed on the top and they don't look wet.
  8. If they spread too much you could use a spatula and push the edges into a circle.
